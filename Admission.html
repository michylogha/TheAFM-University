Admission

Basic Addmission Requirements

Incoming Freshman

  • High School certificate
  • Birth Certificate
  • Good Moral Character Certificate

Returning Students

  • School records from Register
  • Account slip
  • Letter from parents or guidian

Foreign Students

  • Transcript of records
  • National Intelligence Coordinating Agency (NICA) Clearance
  • Quarantine Certificate
  • Notice of Acceptance (NOA)
  • Notarized Bureau of Immigration Form
  • Notarized Authorization for processing of Student Visa by the School

Transferees

  • Certificate of Honorable Dismissal or Transfer Credentials
  • True Copy of Scholastic Records
  • Good Moral Character Certificate

Graduate School

  • Official Transcript of Records with Special Order
  • True Copy of Grades
  • PRC License for Board Exams
  • Entrance Examination Results (for MSCJE)
